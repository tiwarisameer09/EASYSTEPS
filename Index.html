Brief Introduction

Welcome, to Easy Steps, the school with a difference…..

Easy Steps is born out of an overwhelming aspiration to build, provide and nurture an environment that not only inculcates the basic human, cultural and social values in children but also caters to their all round development thereby transforming their childhood into a well-mannered responsible youth.



Our Philosophy

Promoted by a professionally qualified management, the school brings to life the very essence of our underlying philosophy – “knowledge advances by steps, not by leaps.” Guided by our philosophy we aim to provide the young minds, an atmosphere wherein their knowledge and understanding is enhanced step by step through self-paced learning by exposure and first-hand experience.
